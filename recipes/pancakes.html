Old-Fashioned Pancakes

Description:

With this recipe you can make 8 servings of pancakes in 20 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons on baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 1/4 cups of milk
  • 3 tablespoons of butter, melted
  • 1 egg

Method:

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt together in a large bowl. Make a well in the center and add milk, melted butter, and egg; mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  4. Cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
