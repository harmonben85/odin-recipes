Pepperoni Pizza

Description:

This is an easy recipe for a homemade pepperoni pizza. It takes around 30 minutes to prepare. You are free to add any extra toppings if you like.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of pizza dough
  • 15-20 slices of pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup of pizza sauce
  • 6 ounces of mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • extra topping of your choice (sausage, onions, olives, green peppers, etc.)

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle-shape of 14-16 inches wide.
  3. Add a light layer of sauce around the pizza.
  4. Add the grated mozzarella cheese and pepperoni, then lightly season with black pepper.
  5. Bake the pizza in the oven for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Let the pizza cool down for a couples of minutes and serve!
